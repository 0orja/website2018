HIGH SCHOOL STUDENTS

While many of our events and resources are targeted toward NYUAD students, we also strive to involve high school students. As future members of the STEM community, we are eager to introduce high school students to the many possible paths that exist with a STEM degree!
Our most popular event is our WeSTEM High School Conference which is a collaborative, interdisciplinary, and locally engaged initiative, designed to promote diversity and female presence in the STEM fields in and beyond the United Arab Emirates.

ABU DHABI COMMUNITY

Contrary to popular belief, most of our events aren’t limited to WeSTEM university students. Members of the public are more than welcome to join in as spectators or participants! Checkout out our News page to find out more about upcoming events.
